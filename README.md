# MRS
Intelligent, medical records solution that help patients manage their medical historical records, track medications and share medical reports with his/her doctor.
The doctor module, can easily view all medical reports shared by patients.
With an intuitive dashboard, and automated categorization, doctors can gain real-time historical medical insights and make informed decisions. Whether it's a treatment planner, appointment reminder, or appointment tracker. The app will be secure, user-friendly, and scalable for long-term growth.
## Key Features
Patient Management, Doctor Management, Lab Managment, Roles, Medical Recors, Appointments, Enhanced Security and Accessibility
